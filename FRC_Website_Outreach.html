FLL Camps

Additionally, over our summer break Valor worked closely with Laura Welch Bush Elementary Robotics Mentor Christina Morales to coordinate and manage a series of First Lego League summer camps for 4th and 5th graders. The first several camps were focused on working with the kids and getting them familiar with the Lego Mindstorm EV3 Kits and software. The last couple of camps were focused on setting up the new Hydrodynamics FLL game and acclimating them to the field and game mechanics. The final summer camp involved showcasing our STEAMworks Off-Season robot as an opportunity to inspire young minds as well as ViperBots sustainability and recruitment.

FTC Kickoff

And one of the last offseason outreach opportunities Valor was given was to host the FTC Relic Recovery Kickoff event that Vandegrift hosts every year. Traditionally the entire ViperBots organization, which was previously composed of only FTC teams, was in charge of planning and running the event.
