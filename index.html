About Us

The home of Team Valor is at Vandegrift High School in Austin, Texas. The school was built in 2009, with a name that strives to embody Lieutenant Matthew Ryan Vandegrift. Lt. Vandegrift was a Marine stationed in Iraq for eight brutal months between 2007 and 2008. Matthew’s story plays a large factor in how the members of the Vandegrift ViperBots conduct themselves. Therefore, the fifteen original members of this team found it fitting to name themselves after Matthew’s valor; his courage in the face of danger. The team met on May 8th, 2017 for the first time and immediately after named themselves “ViperBots Valor”.

Outreach

Over our summer break Valor worked closely with Laura Welch Bush Elementary Robotics Mentor Christina Morales to coordinate and manage a series of First Lego League summer camps for 4th and 5th graders. Additionally, Valor was given the opportunity to host the FTC Relic Recovery Kickoff event that Vandegrift hosts every year.
